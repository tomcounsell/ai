What This Book Is About

The workforce is changing. AI agents are no longer theoretical abstractions — they are teammates, collaborators, and specialists embedded in real organizations doing real work.

Blended Workforce 2026 is for the CEO who knows AI is transforming their industry but needs a practical playbook — not more hype. Each chapter tackles a specific challenge: hiring your first AI employee, restructuring teams for human-AI collaboration, measuring performance across silicon and carbon, and building a culture where both thrive.

Published as a serialized field manual, each edition captures what actually works right now — because in this landscape, last year’s playbook is already outdated.
